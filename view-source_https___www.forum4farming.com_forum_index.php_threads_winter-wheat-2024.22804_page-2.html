midlands,,, we waited until october last year, but shur it rained nearly all of october ended up sowing november. so we were thinking of going in late sept.
Click to expand...
As @gone says, you will be loading on the Septoria pressure by going before October. That said, there’s a fair yield penalty if you have pieces of fields missing if you get caught by weather.
Be prepared to go in hard after Septoria with your T1 & T2, no watery cheapo mixes.

Some people do plant wheat before barley if the wheat is following a break crop in order to lessen the impact of Take All on the barley.
